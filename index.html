Ticks are a common issue in North America.

A tick bite can infect humans and animals with bacteria, viruses and protozoans that can cause diseases.

What to do after removing a tick?

  • Clean the bite area and your hands with rubbing alcohol or soap and water
  • Dispose of the tick by flushing it down the toilet OR bring the tick to your healthcare provider for identification(place it in a sealed bag/container.)
  • Go visit your healthcare provider
